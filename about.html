Our Story

Welcome to Corsica Bar & Restaurant, your premier dining destination in the heart of Embu. Since opening our doors, we've been committed to serving delicious, quality food in a warm and welcoming atmosphere.

We believe that great food brings people together. Whether you're here for a quick lunch, a family dinner, or drinks with friends, we're dedicated to making every visit memorable.

Our chefs use fresh, locally-sourced ingredients to create dishes that honor traditional Kenyan flavors while adding our own special touch.
